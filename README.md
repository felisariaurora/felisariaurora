  <h3>👩‍💻 About Me</h3>

  I'm finishing my Master's in Computer Science at the University of Parma, with a focus on AI (expected graduation Q3 2026).

  I got here through an unusual path: a linguistic high school, three foreign languages, then a switch into software engineering. That background turns out to be useful, especially in anything that involves human-machine interaction.

  My thesis builds on a multi-agent conversational system I designed and built during a curricular internship at **OT Consulting**. The system helps employees retrieve internal data and automate workflows through natural language, running on GCP with Vertex AI.
